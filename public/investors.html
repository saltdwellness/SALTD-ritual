The Solution
One stick.
Real hydration.
SALTD. is a precision electrolyte powder in a tear-open stick. Drop it in water — any water — and transform it into functional hydration formulated for India's climate, with a proprietary blend of electrolytes, ashwagandha, and ginseng.
Science-backed electrolyte formulaSodium, Potassium, Magnesium, Calcium — ratios tuned for Indian heat and sweat loss
Proprietary adaptogen blendAshwagandha + ginseng — stress resilience and cognitive support. A formulation no competitor has.
Zero sugar, clean labelNo artificial sweeteners, dyes, or fillers — built for daily ritual, not once-a-year sport
Made for everyoneThe office worker, the runner, the mother — not just athletes. A habit product, not a supplement.
